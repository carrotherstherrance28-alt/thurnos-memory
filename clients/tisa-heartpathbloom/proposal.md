### Phase 1 — Production Foundation
**Timeline:** 6–8 weeks  
**Setup fee:** $3,500  
**Monthly retainer:** $500/month

**What gets built:**

**1. Base-44 → Production Migration**
Heart Path Bloom currently lives on Base-44, a prototyping tool. It's perfect for what you needed — proof of concept and visual clarity. But it can't handle real users at scale, and it can't be audited for compliance. We migrate the full app to a production stack (React + secure backend) that you own completely.

**2. Legal & Compliance Layer**
Before collecting any data from minors, three documents are legally required:
- Privacy Policy (COPPA-compliant — parental consent flow for under-13)
- Terms of Service
- Safeguarding / Mandatory Disclosure Policy (when the app must notify a trusted adult regardless of privacy settings)

This isn't bureaucracy — it's what unlocks school partnerships and grant eligibility.

**3. Emotion Vocabulary Expansion**
Your check-in currently offers 5 moods (Great, Okay, Meh, Low, Struggling). We expand this with a nuanced emotion labeling layer — drawn from grief-specific research — so teens can name what they're actually feeling, not just how intense it is. This is a key clinical differentiator no competitor has.

**4. Crisis Safety Hardening**
The crisis resources are correct. Phase 1 adds the infrastructure behind them: automatic escalation logic, trusted adult notification flow, and a mandatory disclosure trigger that protects both the user and the platform legally.

### Phase 2 — Growth Layer
**Timeline:** 6–10 weeks after Phase 1  
**Setup fee:** $3,000–$4,000 (scoped after Phase 1)  
**Monthly retainer:** Continues at $500/month

**What gets built:**

**1. Therapist Backend**
The $30/2-week session model you've priced is the right model. Phase 2 builds the infrastructure behind it: therapist onboarding, scheduling, session matching, and a provider network. We'll scope therapist sourcing together — this is a partnership we build, not a problem you solve alone.

**2. Peer / Community Layer**
The original concept included peer connection — teens supporting teens through grief. This is the feature that creates long-term retention and community. Phase 2 builds moderated peer groups, anonymous sharing, and community affirmations — all with appropriate safeguarding.

**3. Guardian Portal**
A weekly summary for parents/guardians — no raw journal access, consent-gated, mood trend visibility only. This is what makes Heart Path Bloom sellable to schools and districts.

**4. Analytics & Reporting**
Engagement data, mood trend dashboards, and clinical export formats — the data layer that makes you credible to mental health professionals and grant evaluators.
