## Compliance Flags
- COPPA — parental consent flow required for under-13
- Safeguarding/mandatory disclosure policy — legally required
- Privacy policy + ToS — must exist before collecting any minor data
- AI chat (if added) must be co-developed with licensed mental health professional
- BAA may be required if HIPAA-adjacent therapy workflows are involved
